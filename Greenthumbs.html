Crop Calendar

Here is a sample guide for planting:

  • January - March: Start planting rice and vegetables like eggplant and tomato.
  • April - June: Ideal for corn and mango harvesting.
  • July - September: Plant leafy vegetables and monitor for typhoons.
  • October - December: Prepare soil, good for root crops like sweet potato.
